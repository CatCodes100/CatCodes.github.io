Recipe #1: Berry Blast

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Berry Blast!
Ingredients Quantity
blackberries one pint
raspberries one pint
blueberries one pint
ice as needed
milk two cups

Instructions:

Step 1: Put all ingredients in a blender.

Step 2: Blend and enjoy.



Recipe#2: The Best Braised Brussels Sprouts!

  • Time: Less than 30 minutes!
  • Serves: 4
The Best Braised Brussels Sprouts
Ingredients Quantity
fresh brussels sprouts 1 pound
olive oil enough to drizzle
parmesan cheese as desired
garlic to taste

Instructions:

Step 1: Clean and trim sprouts.

Step 2: Arrange in a single layer on a roasting pan.

Step 3: Add olive oil.

Step 4: Add garlic.

Step 5: Add parmesan cheese.

Step 6: Add salt and pepper.

Step 7: Roast at 400 degrees for 20 mins.

Source: The Best Brussel Sprouts of Your Life



Recipe #3: Fresh Ground Coffee

  • Time: 15 mins.
  • Serves: 1
Fresh Ground Coffee
Ingredients Quantity
coffee beans 3 TBS
water 3/4 cup
salt (opt.) a pinch

Instructions:

Step 1: Grind beans.

Step 2: Boil the water.

Step 3: Allow the coffee grounds to bloom.

Step 4: Brew the coffee.

Step 5: Strain out the grounds.

Source: Fresh Coffee
